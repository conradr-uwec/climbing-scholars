Our Story

Growing up, Evelyn May knew she wanted to work with children. She babysat her cousins, and was often referred to as "Miss May" by her family, even as a child. After teaching for 5 years at a local elementary school, Evelyn knew she could do something more. Climbing Scholars was born as a place for children to learn and grow in a welcoming, safe environment. Climbing Scholars is now run by Evelyn May's daughter, Priscilla May, but Evelyn still visits often.

Core Values

Here at Climbing Scholars, we practice a set of core values that are the root of our success:

  • Trust
  • Honesty
  • Love
  • Compassion

These values are how we teach your children to grow confidently. We practice what we preach, and set good examples for your kids to learn from. We teach real world ideals with love and care, so that your children are prepared for their future.
